Biography

Thiago leads the Data Science Team at MPRJ. Prior to joining MPRJ he was a distinguished software developer at several companies. Thiago holds a degree in Computer Science from the Federal University of Juiz de Fora, a master's degree and a doctorate in Informatics from the Federal University of the State of Rio de Janeiro.


Moreover, he informally collaborates with the Semantic and Learning Research Group and his main interest is to understand large scale learning.
